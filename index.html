GR20

The GR20 is a legendary long-distance hiking trail that crosses the rugged mountains of Corsica from north to south. Known as one of the toughest treks in Europe, it offers breathtaking views, challenging climbs, and diverse landscapes. Spanning about 180 km, it attracts adventure seekers from around the world.

Equipment

When hiking, it's essential to have proper footwear suited to the terrain to prevent injuries and ensure comfort. You should also always carry water, a map or GPS, and a first aid kit to be prepared for any situation.

GR30

The GR30 is a scenic circular hiking trail in the Auvergne region of central France, known for its volcanic landscapes and serene lakes. It winds through the heart of the Parc naturel régional des Volcans d’Auvergne, offering views of ancient volcanoes and picturesque villages. This moderate trek is perfect for nature lovers seeking tranquility and natural beauty.
